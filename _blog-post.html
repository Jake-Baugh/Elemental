Medilogik’s Endoscopy Management System (EMS) is an endoscopy reporting and imaging solution with integrated scheduling and booking to help endoscopy teams work smarter and more productively and assist in managing waiting times.

Brief

Faced with substantial market opportunity for its EMS solution, Medilogik asked Elemental to help upgrade its marketing materials to initially support lead generation at a key industry exhibition, and re-use for future promotional campaigns.

Approach

Elemental worked closely with Medilogik to understand how it wanted to be positioned to its target audiences before providing design recommendations and managing the print production process creating refreshed marketing collateral.
